eBox — Outreach Strategy

Conference Lead
Outreach Plan

Feb 2026
For Internal Review
Objective
Convert conference leads into booked eForms demo calls using a 2-touch email sequence plus coordinated LinkedIn outreach.

The Asset

Conference Lead List

A warm, targeted list of companies already aware of the problem eForms solves. The shared conference context gives us a natural, credible opening with no cold pitching required.

Why Now

Warm Leads, Tight Window

Conference connections go cold fast. Reaching out now while the memory is still fresh maximises response rates and keeps the brand top of mind before competitors move in.

Channel 01 — Email

2-Touch Email Sequence

  • Email 1: Warm intro, conference tie-in, introduce eForms, soft CTA to website
  • Email 2 (3–5 days later): Follow-up, address a specific pain point, direct CTA to book a demo
  • Short, plain-text style for both emails

Channel 02 — LinkedIn

Connection + Message

  • Connect with all leads using team profiles
  • Personalised connection note referencing the conference
  • After connect accepted: short follow-up pointing to demo or website
  • Runs in parallel with the email sequence

End Goal

Book the Demo

  • All touchpoints direct to the eBox website (going live soon)
  • Primary CTA: book a demo call
  • Secondary CTA: learn more or watch a product overview

Core Messaging — What We're Saying Across All Touchpoints

The Hook

"We both attended the same conference. Wanted to reach out while that was still fresh."

The Value Prop

"We built eForms to solve exactly the problem that came up at the event. Think it could be a fit for your team."

The Ask

"Would you be open to a quick 20-minute demo? Happy to show you how it works with no pressure."

The Fallback

"In the meantime, feel free to take a look at what we have built: [eBox website URL]."
